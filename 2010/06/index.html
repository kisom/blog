the beginnings of a guide to pf

2010-06-02 by kisom, tagged as guide, networking, pf

I started writing a guide to OpenBSD's pf guide. Courtesy of qbit, vlado, and carmel, I've got a number of topics to cover including:

  • anchors
  • bandwidth limiting
  • OpenBGP load balancing
  • server load balancing

The guide will be updated here ; it's far from being ready for general dissemination but if you'd care to proofread it, I'd appreciate feedback to either my devio.us email or personal email address. It mostly gets written between 01:00 and 05:00 so I'm sure there's plenty of errors. At this point, I'm mostly interested in dumping information and then proofreading it later but I'm not opposed to getting feedback before it's finished.

It's being written in vim, so it's much easier to deal with than the Beginner's Guide (being written in OpenOffice).
